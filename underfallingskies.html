Game Randomiser

Randomises a game set-up using all campaign contents. Toggling 'Use Characters' or 'Use Mission' will often lead to harder feeling games as more sky tiles will be flipped, even though the difficulty score will be properly balanced to the desired level.

Click on the "Copy to Clipboard" button at the bottom to get the game set-up as formatted text, for add as a comment in BGG play logs.
